Process

When I begin work on a new project for a business I start by researching their online persona and existing sales funnel to better understand how the new site can improve their conversion rate.

I help the company build a story with client testimonials and imagery. This emotional connection creates an environment for site visitor to get a sense of the experience of working with the business.


I also research keywords to improve SEO/SERP and integrate these into the site's data structure.

Roles Include:

  • Web Design & Development
  • HTML,CSS & Javascript
  • Site Redesign/Mirgration & Hosting
  • UX/UI
  • CRO & SEO/SERP
  • Logo
  • Branding
  • Graphics
